Rupiha

What is
Rupiha?

Web3 platform

Rupiha is a Web3 platform for all real estate stakeholders, which provides users with the ability to manage interactions between physical and digital assets through unique, and collectively generated, 3D representations of their buildings.

Rupiha uses blockchain technology to record essential data, which enables transparent, traceable, and immutable streams of information to be readily accessible to its users. Magma smart contracts are built with this blockchain data to expedite tasks like requests for proposals, lease agreements, and services arrangements.
